## 📌 Project Overview

Developed an interactive **Power BI dashboard** to analyze and compare **World and Asian fuel prices** from **2015–2026**. The dashboard provides insights into fuel price trends, diesel and gasoline prices, oil dependency, CO₂ emissions, income levels, and tax burden across countries.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Compare fuel prices across Asian and non-Asian countries.
- Analyze diesel and gasoline price trends.
- Evaluate the impact of tax burden on fuel prices.
- Monitor CO₂ emissions from transport.
- Identify regional differences in fuel affordability and income.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards for average fuel, diesel, and gasoline prices
- Country-wise fuel price comparison
- Tax burden category analysis
- Average monthly income vs diesel price
- CO₂ transport emission analysis
- Waterfall chart for crude oil price trends
- Diesel vs gasoline price comparison
- Region-wise fuel price table
- Interactive filters and slicers

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Asian countries show significant variation in fuel prices due to differences in taxation and economic conditions.
- Countries with higher average income generally experience better fuel affordability.
- Transport CO₂ emissions vary considerably across countries, with a few countries contributing disproportionately.
- Diesel and gasoline prices differ across regions based on government policies and market conditions.
